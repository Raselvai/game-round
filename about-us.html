Roadmap

AN NFT

YOUR DISTINCTIVE NFT WILL BE YOUR AVATAR IN OUR GOONIEZ GANG RACING
GAME. THE BETA VERSION WILL LAUNCH IN Q3 2022. ALL GAME ASSETS HAVE
BEEN IN DEVELOPMENT FOR FOUR MONTHS.

3D COLLECTIBLES

THE GOONIEZ COMMUNITY WILL VOTE TO CHOOSE FOUR NFTS FROM THE
COLLECTION TO TURN INTO 3D COLLECTIBLES. THESE LIMITED EDITION
COLLECTIBLES WILL BE SOLD ONLY TO GOONIEZ HOLDERS, WHILE A SELECT
FEW WILL BE GIFTED.

MERCH

THE GOONIEZ GANG MERCHANDISE STORE WILL UNLOCK MULTIPLE DROPS
THROUGHOUT THE YEAR. 25% OF ALL SALES PROCEEDS WILL BE ADDED TO THE
GOONIEZ COMMUNITY WALLET. THIS WALLET WILL BE DEDICATED TO FUNDING
GOONIEZ GANG COMMUNITY FUNCTIONS.

OWNERSHIP

YOU WILL OWN YOUR GOONIEZ OBJ AND FBX FILES. THESE FILES WILL BE
HANDED OVER TO YOU WITH ALL INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY RIGHTS TO YOUR
CHARACTER.

EARN

YOU WILL HAVE THE OPPORTUNITY TO EARN $GANG TOKENS, OUR OFFICIAL IN-
GAME TOKEN. MORE INFORMATION WILL BE ANNOUNCED SOON- STAY TUNED!

ANIMATED SERIES

AS THE GOONIEZ GANG TAKES OVER THE METAVERSE, OUR CAMERAS WILL BE
THERE TO CAPTURE ALL THE ACTION. OUR ANIMATED SERIES WILL PROVIDE YOU
WITH AN IN-DEPTH LOOK AT THE GOONIEZ ORIGIN AND STORY LEADING UP TO
THE GAME.

SERIES HINT: OTHER NFT PROJECTS BEWARE… WE ARE COMING TO CAUSE
CHAOS!

EVENTS

THERE WILL BE EXCLUSIVE EXPERIENCES FOR TOKEN HOLDERS. SOON AFTER
MINT, THERE WILL BE A RAFFLE AMONG GOONIEZ HOLDERS FOR PAID TRAVEL
TO MEET THE TEAM, RECORD CONTENT AND MORE.
